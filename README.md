This project was developed as the CW for AML course. 

Prject Goal: 
Predicting the spatial distribution of bird species by in-tegrating climatic factors. To achieve this, the geographic coordinates of bird distribution were linked to corresponding climatic variables (precipitation and min-imum/maximum temperature) derived from each location’s longitude and latitude. 
LightGBM was found to be the best performing model in comparison to Logistic Regression and Random Forest, achieving a mean ROC-AUC of 0.88. Finally, we were able to predict the most probable bird species at a given location.
